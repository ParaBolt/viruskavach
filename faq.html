FAQ - Virus Kavach AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant (VK-AFD)

How VK AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant works?

  • VK-AFD creates AntiViral nano coating on the fabric
  • AntiViral nano coating inactivates virus and bacteria on contact

Are there any harmful chemicals in VK AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant?

  • VK-AFD is made from derivatives of organic citric acid combined with proprietary fabric coating technology (Patent pending).
  • VK-AFD has FDA License Approval and all ingredients are FDA approved.

How VK AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant is different from others?

  • Other Disinfectant - Most disinfectant are alcohol basedand and once it evaporates from surface, they are unable to provide any protection against virus and bacteria
  • VK-AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant - Creates AntiViral nano coating which provides continuous protection against virus and bacterial for 48 hours.
  • The coating remains active for a minimum of 48 hours on the fabric and inactivates the virus and bacteria in 15 min contact time.

How long VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant provides protection?

  • Once applied, the AntiViral nano coating remains active for 48 hours on fabric or unless it is washed.
  • After application AntiViral nano coating inactivates the virus and bacteria in 15 min contact time.

Where VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant can be applied?

  • Can be applied on

    • Regular Clothes like Shirt, Pant, Dress, Apron, Jacket
    • Fabric / Cotton Mask
    • Apron, Gloves, PPE Suit
    • Car Seat
    • Sofa, Curtains etc

  • Cannot be applied on

    • Leather
    • Silk or
    • Fabric that may water spot

Can we apply VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant on Fabric Mask?

  • VK-AFD can be applied on the outermost layer, prone to contamination.
  • VK-AFD provides excellent additional protection layer for masks.

How to use VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant on Clothes?

  • Spray VK-AFD on clean dry clothes until slightly wet / damp, so that even AntiViral nano coating is created on clothes
  • Let the clothes dry for 5 mins and they are ready to use. Optionally you can iron the clothes as well.
  • VK-AFD can also be applied to ironed clothes until slightly wet / damp. Let the clothes dry for 5 mins and they are ready to use.

Can we apply VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant on Fabric cover of the chair or seat?

Yes, you can apply it on the Fabric cover of the chair, sofa or seat when you are traveling or in office or at other place.

  • Spray VK-AFD on fabric cover until slightly wet / damp, so that even AntiViral nano coating is created.
  • Let the fabric cover dry and they are ready to use. Optionally use can steam iron the fabric cover as well.

Can we apply VK - AntiViral Fabric Disinfectant on the Skin or any Body part?

No. Please do not apply on Skin or any body part.

  • This spray is developed for fabrics only.
  • It can cause mild irritation to the eyes.
